Our Story

The Fusion of Fine Dining & Recreation

Appetito Club was born out of a simple concept: why should dining out be limited to just sitting at a table? We designed a contemporary space where food meets action, vibes meet comfort, and people build connections.

Located in the heart of Knowledge Park II, Greater Noida, we feature an exquisite multi-cuisine restaurant, board game libraries, live music events, and full-scale outdoor recreation facilities including Pickleball and Mini Golf.
