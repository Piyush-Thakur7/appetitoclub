Who We Are

A Place Crafted For Connections

Appetito Club was established with a singular vision: to break the monotony of the traditional dining model and offer a refreshing, interactive venue for people of all ages. We believe dining is not just about the food on the plate; it is about the conversations, the smiles, and the memories shared around the table.

We designed our spacious venue in Greater Noida to blend premium gourmet dining with dynamic, active recreational experiences. Whether you are catching up with old friends over a heated board game, celebrating a milestone with live music, or keeping active on our pickleball courts, Appetito Club is your space.

Why We Are Different

Beyond Just A Restaurant

At Appetito Club, we integrate several lifestyle features to deliver a holistic experience:

  • Handcrafted Culinary Menu

    Our ingredients are locally sourced, fresh, and whipped into exquisite multi-cuisine plates by master chefs.

  • Recreational Adventure Areas

    Host to Greater Noida's premier Bikonic Pump Track, ParView Mini Golf course, and PickleNow Pickleball courts.

  • Vibrant Culture

    We celebrate music, community, and social sports with weekly karaoke leagues, board game events, and DJ nights.
